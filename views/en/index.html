IBM User Group Conference！

Overview

Today, the IBM Cloud and OSS from IBM are constantly evolving and contributing to development. In line with this trend, many user groups have been publishing various information daily. The conference provides an opportunity for these User Groups to come together, get up-to-date on the latest information, and learn from each other. The purpose of this event is to communicate with everyone involved in technology and to disseminate information.

The conference will be co-sponsored by various IBM user communities.We make the opportunity to make interactions between each community, catching up on technology information in each other.Let's get the whole IBM user community going based on activity sharing and more!

Appeal point

If you are interested in and use IBM's technology, products and services, we encourage you to attend and participate in this event. Let's do it!
The conference is packed with information from various IBM user communities.

  • Frontline members from each user community will be speaking at this event. There will be a lot of latest information and activities, as well as technical sessions.
  • As the name implies, a user community is a group of users who use the same service or product Location. This is your chance to join the community.
  • The conference is hosted by the Japanese community, but as only digital technology can do, it's a great opportunity for the international community to get involved. We will also welcome speakers and audiences.
  • There will be a lot of experts from all walks of life in attendance. Get great inspiration.
  • The user community tends to be a silo. Let’s interact with other communities.
  • The appeal of the conference is to meet people who share the same goals. Connect with other attendees both at home and abroad.
